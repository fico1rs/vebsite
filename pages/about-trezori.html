About Trezori

Trezori is my way of sharing all my ideas with the world, in one place. I have projects I've been working on from Guides -> VCs -> Crypto -> Motorcycle Insurance.
I hope what I create can help some people, and that it'll be shared with the world.

Unfortunately it's hard to balance school and projects but I do my best to consistently work on and push my new project ideas.
Eventually I plan on creating enough projects where my ideas run out and I focus heavily on just a few.

As of today only the Belgrade Tourist Guide has been uploaded but I have 2-3 more that are near completion and ready.
